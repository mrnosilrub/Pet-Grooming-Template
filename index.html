Meet Lacy & The Shack

Hi! I’m Lacy, a life‑long animal lover & certified professional groomer. I opened The Groom Shack to give anxious dogs and cats a quiet, safe haven away from the bustle of big‑box salons. Your pet enjoys a private, cage‑free appointment where they get my full attention from start to finish.

From fluffy doodles to nervous rescues, every client leaves wagging, purring and looking their absolute best.
